Allah Helps Those Who Help Themselves

A mosque, or masjid, is a dedicated place of worship for Muslims, serving as a center for prayer, community gathering, and education. Originating from the Prophet Muhammad's home in Medina, key architectural features often include a minaret for the call to prayer, a dome, and a mihrab (niche) indicating the qibla direction towards Mecca
